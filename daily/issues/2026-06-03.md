### [Mitigating Perceptual Judgment Bias in Multimodal LLM-as-a-Judge via Perceptual Perturbation and Reward Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02578v1)
**Authors:** Seojeong Park, Jiho Choi, Junyong Kang, Seonho Lee, Jaeyo Shin, Hyunjung Shim
**Published:** 2026-06-01
**Summary:** MLLM judges favor plausible text over visual facts; a perturbation dataset and reward model fix this perceptual bias.

**What's new**
- Identifies Perceptual Judgment Bias where MLLM judges ignore visual evidence for plausible text.
- Creates Perceptually Perturbed Judgment Dataset with counterfactual responses for verifiable supervision.
- Proposes unified training with GRPO reward and batch-ranking for coherent ordering without pairwise labels.
- Shows improved perceptual fidelity, ranking coherence, and human alignment across benchmarks.

### [AdaCodec: A Predictive Visual Code for Video MLLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02569v1)
**Authors:** Haowen Hou, Zhen Huang, Zheming Liang, Qingyi Si, Chenglin Li, Shuai Dong, Kele Shao, Ruilin Li, Dianyi Wang, Nan Duan, Jiaqi Wang
**Published:** 2026-06-01
**Summary:** AdaCodec reduces video redundancy for MLLMs by sending full frames only when needed, using compact P-tokens for inter-frame changes.

**What's new**
- Predictive visual code that transmits full reference frames only when prediction cost is high.
- Encodes motion and prediction residuals as compact P-tokens instead of full RGB frames.
- Matches or surpasses per-frame RGB baseline at 1/7 the visual token budget.
- Cuts time-to-first-token from 9.26s to 1.62s on general-video benchmarks.

### [ClinEnv: An Interactive Multi-Stage Long Horizon EHR Environment for Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02568v1)

**Published:** 2026-06-01
**Summary:** ClinEnv is an interactive benchmark evaluating LLMs as physicians in multi-stage inpatient simulations, revealing a gap between decision outcomes and information-gathering process.

**What's new**
- Introduces Longitudinal Inpatient Simulation with ordered decision stages and four specialized agents.
- Scores both decision outcomes (via ontology-grounded matching) and information-gathering process.

### [HERO'S JOURNEY: Testing Complex Rule Induction with Text Games](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02556v1)

**Published:** 2026-06-01

**What's new**

### [Symmetry-Protected Weyl Nodal Loops in a Triangular Altermagnet](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02527v1)
**Authors:** Chao-Chun Wei, Xiaoyin Li, Sophia Adams, Jacob Kjeldahl Jensen, Qiang Zhang, Jue Liu, Maxim Avdeev, Dinesh Kumar Yadav, Vikram V. Deshpande, Luisa Whittaker-Brooks, Feng Liu, Huiwen Ji
**Published:** 2026-06-01
**Summary:** Cr7Se8 realizes a Weyl nodal-loop altermagnet with coplanar 120° magnetic order and mirror-protected nodal loops near the Fermi level.

**What's new**
- First realization of a Weyl nodal-loop altermagnet in Cr7Se8.
- Coplanar 120° compensated magnetic order on a triangular lattice breaks inversion-time and translation-time reversal symmetries.
- Mirror symmetry protects continuous Weyl-like nodal loops in the k_z=0 plane.
- Near Fermi level, crossings form Dirac-like fourfold degeneracies without spin-orbit coupling.
- Momentum-dependent spin polarization shows f-wave-like pattern characteristic of odd-parity altermagnets.
