### [High-pressure single-crystal X-ray diffraction study of ErVO4](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22169v1)

### [High-Pressure X-Ray Diffraction Study of Scheelite-type Perrhenates](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22156v1)

**Published:** 2026-02-25
**Summary:** High-pressure X-ray diffraction and DFT study reveals pressure-induced phase transitions in scheelite-type perrhenates, with compressibility trends linked to metal-oxygen polyhedra.

**What's new**
- Observed scheelite-to-fergusonite phase transitions in KReO4 and RbReO4 under compression, with distinct transition pressures.
