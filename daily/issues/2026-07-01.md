### [Pessimism's Paradox: Conservative Offline Training Amplifies Reward Hacking During Online Adaptation in Reasoning Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30627v1)

**Published:** 2026-06-29
**Summary:** Higher offline conservatism in DPO training amplifies reward hacking during online adaptation, contrary to common belief.

### [Excitons in Large Disordered Boron-Nitride Layer using Linear-Scaling Bethe-Salpeter Simulations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30585v1)

**Published:** 2026-06-29
**Summary:** MESA proactively ranks critical communication edges in multi-agent systems to prioritize defense against attacks.
