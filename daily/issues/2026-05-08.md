Today's papers span AI safety and alignment, generative model improvements, and materials science advances. ### [Taming Outlier Tokens in Diffusion Transformers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05206v1)
**Authors:** Xiaoyu Wu, Yifei Wang, Tsu-Jui Fu, Liang-Chieh Chen, Zhe Gan, Chen Wei
**Published:** 2026-05-06
**Summary:** Outlier tokens in Diffusion Transformers degrade image generation; Dual-Stage Registers mitigate them.

**What's new**
- Identifies outlier tokens in both ViT encoders and DiT denoisers of RAE-DiT pipelines.
- Shows masking high-norm tokens fails, linking outliers to corrupted local patch semantics.
- Proposes Dual-Stage Registers (DSR) with trained, test-time, and diffusion registers.

### [The First Token Knows: Single-Decode Confidence for Hallucination Detection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05166v1)
**Authors:** Mina Gabriel
**Published:** 2026-05-06
**Summary:** First-token confidence matches or exceeds semantic self-consistency for hallucination detection, offering a low-cost baseline.

**What's new**
- Proposes phi_first: normalized entropy of top-K logits at the first answer token of a single greedy decode.
- Achieves mean AUROC 0.820 vs 0.793 for semantic self-consistency across models and benchmarks.
- Shows phi_first is moderately to strongly correlated with semantic agreement, with minimal gain from combining both.
- Argues phi_first should be a default low-cost baseline before using sampling-based uncertainty estimation.

### [The Impossibility Triangle of Long-Context Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05066v1)
**Authors:** Yan Zhou
**Published:** 2026-05-06
**Summary:** A fundamental trade-off proves no long-context model can simultaneously achieve efficiency, compactness, and full recall.

**What's new**
- Formal proof using Data Processing and Fano's Inequalities for a unified model abstraction.
- Bounds recall capacity to O(poly(d)/log V) for efficient and compact models.
- Classifies 52 architectures into the impossibility triangle, each missing at least one property.
- Hybrid architectures trace continuous trajectories inside the triangle, not escaping it.
- Experiments on synthetic tasks confirm empirical recall lies below the theoretical limit.

### [Local Intrinsic Dimension Unveils Hallucinations in Diffusion Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05026v1)
**Authors:** Bartlomiej Sobieski, Matthew Tivnan, Dawid Płudowski, Michał Jan Włodarczyk, Pengfei Jin, Przemyslaw Biecek, Quanzheng Li
**Published:** 2026-05-06
**Summary:** Diffusion model hallucinations are linked to manifold instabilities driven by local intrinsic dimension; Intrinsic Quenching reduces them.

**What's new**
- Hallucinations in diffusion models are framed as instabilities on the model-induced manifold.
- A hallucination filter based on manifold instabilities matches or beats temporal filters.
- Local intrinsic dimension (LID) is identified as the primary driver of these instabilities.
- Intrinsic Quenching (IQ) deflates LID to directly reduce hallucinations.
- IQ outperforms standard baselines and improves anatomical consistency in medical imaging.

### [KernelBench-X: A Comprehensive Benchmark for Evaluating LLM-Generated GPU Kernels](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04956v1)

**Published:** 2026-05-06
**Summary:** KernelBench-X evaluates LLM-generated GPU kernels across 176 tasks, revealing task structure drives correctness more than method, and correctness doesn't guarantee efficiency.

**What's new**
- Task structure explains 9.4% of correctness variance vs 3.3% for method; 72% of Fusion tasks fail across all methods.
