### [Loc3R-VLM: Language-based Localization and 3D Reasoning with Vision-Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18002v1)

### [Unified Spatio-Temporal Token Scoring for Efficient Video VLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18004v1)
**Authors:** Jianrui Zhang, Yue Yang, Rohun Tripathi, Winson Han, Ranjay Krishna, Christopher Clark, Yong Jae Lee, Sangho Lee
**Published:** 2026-03-18
**Summary:** STTS is a lightweight module that prunes 50% of vision tokens across both vision and language model components in video VLMs, boosting efficiency by 62% with minimal performance loss.

**What's new**
- Unified pruning across both ViT and LLM without text conditioning or token merging, unlike prior unimodal or LLM-only methods.
- Learns scoring via temporal auxiliary loss and spatial LLM gradients, enabling end-to-end training and compatibility.
- Achieves 62% efficiency gain with only 0.7% average performance drop on 13 video QA tasks via a 50% token prune.
- Efficiency improves with more frames; test-time scaling for long videos yields extra 0.5-1% performance gains.
