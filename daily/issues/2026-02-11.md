### [Conditional Sequence Modeling for Safe Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.08584v1)

**Published:** 2026-02-09

**What's new**

### [StealthRL: Reinforcement Learning Paraphrase Attacks for Multi-Detector Evasion of AI-Text Detectors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.08934v1)
**Authors:** Suraj Ranganath, Atharv Ramesh
**Published:** 2026-02-09
**Summary:** StealthRL uses reinforcement learning to generate adversarial paraphrases that evade multiple AI-text detectors while preserving meaning, exposing critical robustness gaps.

**What's new**
- Introduces a reinforcement learning framework (StealthRL) using GRPO and LoRA on Qwen3-4B to train a paraphrase policy against a multi-detector ensemble.
- Achieves near-zero detection (0.001 TPR) and reduces mean AUROC from 0.74 to 0.27, with a 99.9% attack success rate against three detector families.
