### [Anisotropic Short-Range Order Modulates Ferroelectric Switching in Wurtzite ScAlN Alloys](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18213v1)
**Authors:** Shunda Chen, Xianchao Dong, Xiaochen Jin, Tianshu Li
**Published:** 2026-06-16
**Summary:** Anisotropic short-range order in wurtzite ScAlN alloys significantly modulates ferroelectric switching barriers.

**What's new**
- Short-range order (SRO) is identified as a key microscopic variable affecting switching barriers.
- Wurtzite ScAlN exhibits robust anisotropic SRO, not random alloy behavior.
- SRO suppresses in-plane Sc-N-Sc motifs and enhances columnar mixed-cation chains.
- SRO systematically increases intrinsic switching barrier across compositions.
- Columnar Sc-N-Al-N-Sc motifs are primary structural descriptor for barrier variations.
