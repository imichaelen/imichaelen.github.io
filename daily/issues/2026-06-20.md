### [How Transparent is DiffusionGemma?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.20560v1)

**Published:** 2026-06-18
**Summary:** DiffusionGemma's reasoning transparency is comparable to autoregressive models despite its continuous latent space.

**What's new**
- Introduces variable and algorithmic transparency as two components for analyzing diffusion model interpretability.
