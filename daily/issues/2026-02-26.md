# Daily Issue (JST): 2026-02-26

## What's New Today

**AI Agents Advance in Reasoning, Efficiency, and Safety; Materials Research Explores Altermagnetism and Synthesis**

Today's research spans efficient AI reasoning, robust agentic systems, and novel materials physics. Advances include test-time training reinterpretations, memory-efficient context parallelism, and frameworks for safer, more capable AI agents. - ActiveStructOpt uses active learning with GNNs to efficiently determine atomic structures from target spectra.
