### [Polynomial Speedup in Diffusion Models with the Multilevel Euler-Maruyama Method](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24594v1)
**Authors:** Arthur Jacot
**Published:** 2026-03-25
**Summary:** A multilevel method for diffusion models that reduces sampling cost to that of a single drift evaluation, achieving polynomial speedups in image generation.

**What's new**
- Introduces Multilevel Euler-Maruyama (ML-EM) for SDE/ODE solving using a hierarchy of drift approximators with varying cost/accuracy
- Achieves computational cost of ε^{-γ} for ε-approximation in HTMC regime (γ>2), improving over traditional EM's ε^{-γ-1}
- Applies method to diffusion models by training UNets of increasing sizes as the multilevel hierarchy
- Demonstrates up to 4x speedup on CelebA 64x64 with measured γ≈2.5
- Enables sampling with computational equivalent of single largest UNet evaluation
