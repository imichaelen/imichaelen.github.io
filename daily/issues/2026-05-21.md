### [Itinerant Nature of Spin-Density-Wave Order in Ruddlesden-Popper Nickelates](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20148v1)
**Authors:** Jiong Mei, Tianyang Xie, Kun Jiang
**Published:** 2026-05-19
**Summary:** Magnetism in Ruddlesden-Popper nickelates is itinerant, driven by mirror-selective interband nesting, not local moments.

**What's new**

### [HaorFloodAlert: Deseasonalized ML Ensemble for 72-Hour Flood Prediction in Bangladesh Haor Wetlands](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20167v1)
