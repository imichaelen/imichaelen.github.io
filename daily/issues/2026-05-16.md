### [EntityBench: Towards Entity-Consistent Long-Range Multi-Shot Video Generation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15199v1)
**Authors:** Ruozhen He, Meng Wei, Ziyan Yang, Vicente Ordonez
**Published:** 2026-05-14
**Summary:** EntityBench benchmarks entity consistency in long-range multi-shot video generation with 140 episodes and a memory-augmented baseline.

**What's new**
- Introduces EntityBench, a benchmark of 140 episodes with explicit per-shot entity schedules across easy/medium/hard tiers.
- Proposes a three-pillar evaluation suite for intra-shot quality, prompt alignment, and cross-shot consistency with a fidelity gate.
- Presents EntityMem, a memory-augmented system storing verified per-entity references for improved character fidelity.

**Published:** 2026-05-14
**Summary:** ATLAS uses a single 'functional token' for both agentic and latent visual reasoning, avoiding costly image generation.
