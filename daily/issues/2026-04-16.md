## Featured Papers

### [SceneCritic: A Symbolic Evaluator for 3D Indoor Scene Synthesis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13035v1)
**Authors:** Kathakoli Sengupta, Kai Ao, Paola Cascante-Bonilla
**Published:** 2026-04-14
**Summary:** SceneCritic is a symbolic evaluator for 3D indoor scene layouts, using a spatial ontology to verify coherence, outperforming VLM judges and enabling iterative refinement.

**What's new**
- Introduces SceneCritic, a symbolic evaluator for floor-plan-level layouts based on a structured spatial ontology (SceneOnto).
- SceneOnto aggregates indoor priors from 3D-FRONT, ScanNet, and Visual Genome to verify semantic, orientation, and geometric coherence.
- Provides object-level and relationship-level assessments, identifying specific violations and successful placements.
- Pairs SceneCritic with an iterative refinement test bed using rule-based, LLM, and VLM critics for model improvement.
- Shows SceneCritic aligns better with human judgments than VLM evaluators and that VLM refinement is most effective for corrections.

### [Lightning OPD: Efficient Post-Training for Large Reasoning Models with Offline On-Policy Distillation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13010v1)
**Authors:** Yecheng Wu, Song Han, Hai Cai
**Published:** 2026-04-14
**Summary:** Lightning OPD is an efficient offline post-training method that eliminates the need for a live teacher server by precomputing teacher log-probabilities, matching standard on-policy distillation performance with 4x speedup.

**What's new**
- Identifies teacher consistency as a critical condition for on-policy distillation, showing violations cause irreducible gradient bias
- Proposes offline OPD framework that precomputes teacher log-probabilities over SFT rollouts, removing live teacher server requirement
- Demonstrates Lightning OPD shares the same optimum as standard OPD with bounded gradient discrepancy and implicit regularization
- Achieves 4x speedup over standard OPD, reaching 69.9% on AIME 2024 with Qwen3-8B in just 30 GPU hours
